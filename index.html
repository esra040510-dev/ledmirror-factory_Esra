❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the MOQ for standard products? ▼
Standard MOQ: 50pcs for PVC cabinets, 100pcs for smart mirror cabinets, 500pcs for LED mirrors. OEM/ODM MOQ starts from 100pcs.
How long is the sample lead time? ▼
Sample lead time: 7-10 working days. Bulk production: 25-30 days after sample approval.
Do you support OEM/ODM customization? ▼
Yes, we support custom size, shape, logo, light color, packaging, anti-fog, Bluetooth, touch switch, etc.

Shipping by sea, air, or express (DHL/FedEx). DDP door-to-door available for many countries.
What is the warranty period? ▼
2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. After-sales technical support and spare parts provided.

Yes, for projects ≥1000pcs, special discounts apply. Contact us with your project details.
