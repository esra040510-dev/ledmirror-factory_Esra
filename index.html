❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the MOQ for standard products? ▼
Standard MOQ is 50pcs for PVC cabinets, 100pcs for smart mirror cabinets, and 500pcs for LED mirrors. For OEM/ODM custom orders, MOQ starts from 100pcs.
How long is the sample lead time? ▼
Sample lead time is 7-10 working days. Bulk production takes 25-30 days after sample approval.
Do you support OEM/ODM customization? ▼
Yes, we fully support OEM/ODM services including custom size, shape, logo printing, light color, packaging, and additional functions like anti-fog, Bluetooth, touch switch, etc.
What shipping methods do you offer? ▼
We ship by sea, air, or express (DHL/FedEx). DDP door-to-door service is also available for many countries.
What is the warranty period? ▼
All products come with a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. We provide after-sales technical support and spare parts.
Can I get a bulk discount for large projects? ▼
Yes, for engineering projects and large-volume orders (≥1000pcs), we offer special discounts. Please contact us with your project details.
